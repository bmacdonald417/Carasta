# FAQ And Glossary

## FAQ

### What is Carasta?
Carasta is a social automotive platform that combines Carmunity, Discussions, Messages, profiles, Garage identity, live auctions, and seller support in one system.

### Is Carasta only an auction site?
No. Auctions are a major part of the product, but the platform is intentionally broader than a marketplace.

### What does Carmunity mean?
Carmunity is Carasta's community layer for social participation, identity, following, and ongoing enthusiast connection.

### Why does the site emphasize Discussions?
Discussions is the canonical public term for Carasta's thread-based conversation areas and is used to reduce vocabulary drift.

### Can I use Carasta without buying or selling right away?
Yes. You can explore the Carmunity, follow people, participate in Discussions, and build your profile and Garage identity even if you are not currently in the market.

### Where should I go for help or trust questions?
Start with Resources for guides, glossary terms, and trust pages. If you still need help, use the contact page.

### Are the Terms and Privacy pages final legal documents?
No. They are still draft public structures pending legal review.

## Glossary

### Carasta
The overall platform combining Carmunity, Discussions, Messages, profiles, Garage identity, auctions, and seller tools.

### Carmunity
The community layer for public participation, following, and identity.

### Discussions
The canonical public term for thread-based conversation areas.

### Messages
The direct one-to-one communication layer inside the platform.

### Profile
A user's platform identity surface across community and marketplace participation.

### Garage
The part of the identity model that expresses ownership, projects, and dream-car context.

### Auction
A live marketplace listing where bids, reserve context, and timing mechanics shape the sale flow.

### Reserve state
The public indication of how the current bidding relates to the seller's reserve threshold, where supported.

### Anti-sniping
Auction behavior that extends timing late in a listing to reduce last-second distortion and keep bidding fairer.

### Seller tools
The seller-facing workflows and support surfaces that help present, market, and manage listings on Carasta.
