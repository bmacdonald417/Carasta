# Trust Safety And Help

## What Carasta is responsible for
- Providing the platform surfaces and current public guidance
- Maintaining community and support paths
- Applying moderation and conduct standards through the current policy structure
- Clarifying product concepts so users can participate more confidently

## What Carasta is not claiming
- Not every interaction or private message is guaranteed safe or verified
- Public guides do not replace final legal documents or listing-specific terms
- The platform does not remove the need for user diligence in transactions
- Some legal/trust pages are still draft structures pending formal review

## Community Guidelines
Carasta expects respectful participation, discourages illegal or unsafe behavior, and uses moderation to support platform quality. Community Guidelines explain current public conduct expectations for Carmunity, Discussions, and direct interaction.

## Privacy and Terms status
The Privacy Policy and Terms pages are currently draft structures that clarify the current model and support paths while final legal review is still pending. They are not final legal documents.

## Where to go for help
- Resources: product explanations, glossary terms, buying basics, seller context
- Trust pages: Community Guidelines, Privacy Policy, Terms
- Contact: use the contact page when self-serve help is not enough

## Assistant boundary reminder
Carasta help content is designed to be useful and responsible. It should not be treated as legal advice, a moderation ruling, or a source of account-specific certainty unless the product explicitly supports that scope.
