# Navigation And Help Paths

## Where to start for product understanding
Start with Resources when you need to understand what Carasta is, what Carmunity means, how Discussions or Messages fit in, or how buying and selling work at a general level.

## Where to go for trust and policy questions
Use Trust and Safety when the question is about:
- platform boundaries
- what Carasta is or is not responsible for
- current trust posture
- guidance around safety, moderation, or policy surfaces

## Where to go for direct help
Use Contact when:
- the assistant cannot safely verify the answer
- self-serve help is not enough
- the issue may be account-specific
- support escalation is more appropriate than a general product answer

## Where seller tools live
Seller tools are primarily web-first and live in the seller marketing workspace. A seller usually reaches that area from their profile or seller-facing workspace routes rather than from the public marketing pages.

## Where product surfaces fit
- Resources: public help and platform explanations
- Trust and Safety: trust boundaries and support routing
- FAQ: short direct answers
- Carmunity: social/community participation
- Discussions: thread-based public conversation
- Messages: one-to-one communication
- Auctions: live marketplace activity
- Sell: seller-facing listing and workflow entry
