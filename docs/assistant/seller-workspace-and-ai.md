# Seller Workspace And AI

## Seller growth workspace
The seller marketing area has been redesigned into a seller growth workspace. It is meant to feel more analytical, action-oriented, and premium than the public marketing site.

## What the seller workspace is for
- understanding portfolio health
- seeing listing-level performance context
- reviewing alerts and recent activity
- managing campaigns and share plans
- using AI seller tools in a review-first workflow

## AI seller systems
Carasta currently has two main seller AI systems:
- Listing AI
- AI Marketing Copilot

Both systems are designed to stay human-in-the-loop, structured, validated, and reviewable.

## Listing AI
Listing AI helps sellers draft and refine listing content. It is designed to improve copy, condition framing, missing-information awareness, and disclosure clarity without inventing unsupported facts.

## Marketing Copilot
Marketing Copilot helps sellers generate strategy, tasks, and content drafts for selected channels. It is workflow-aware and reviewable, but it does not autonomously post or execute campaigns.

## Important boundaries
- The AI systems do not replace seller judgment
- They do not guarantee performance outcomes
- They should not invent unsupported facts
- They are designed to help with structured drafting and planning rather than autonomous execution
