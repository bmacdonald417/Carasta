# Identity And Garage

## Why identity matters on Carasta
Carasta treats identity as a product feature. Profiles and Garage help people show who they are, what they own, and what they care about. That makes public participation and marketplace activity feel more grounded.

## Profiles
Profiles connect activity across the platform. The same identity can appear in Carmunity, Discussions, auctions, Messages, and seller surfaces instead of living in separate silos.

## Garage
Garage is the part of the identity model that expresses ownership, projects, and dream-car context. It is not only a cosmetic feature. It supports the broader enthusiast identity model Carasta is built around.

## Trust implications
- Profiles and Garage add continuity and context
- They help users understand who they are interacting with
- They support the idea that community and marketplace belong together
- They do not replace normal user judgment or transaction-specific diligence
