# Auctions Buying And Selling

## Auction basics
Carasta auctions support live bidding. Listings can include reserve context where supported, and anti-sniping behavior helps reduce last-second distortion so bidding stays more legible and fair.

## What buyers should keep in mind
- Read the listing carefully rather than relying on a short summary alone
- Use trust and support pages when you need platform-level guidance
- Do not assume a public guide replaces listing-specific terms or final legal documents
- Use judgment around payment, inspection, condition review, and communication

## Buying on Carasta
Buyers should expect a flow that includes:
- reviewing the listing, seller context, and auction details before bidding
- following the live auction closely during the active window
- using platform guidance and communication paths after the close

## Selling on Carasta
Carasta's seller side is more than a listing form. Sellers benefit from a platform that connects identity, marketplace activity, and audience. The seller story includes listing visibility, auction mechanics, and the broader Carmunity context around a sale.

## Seller tools direction
Carasta is moving toward stronger seller marketing support, analytics, and AI-assisted workflows. Public help content describes that direction carefully and does not imply that every future seller workspace improvement has already shipped.
