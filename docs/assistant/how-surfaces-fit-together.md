# How Surfaces Fit Together

## Carmunity
Carmunity is the community layer. It covers following, public participation, identity, and the broader social feel of the platform.

## Discussions
Discussions are the thread-based public conversation layer. Use Discussions for structured topics, questions, and public conversation that should stay visible and useful to more than one person.

## Messages
Messages are the one-to-one communication layer. Use Messages when a discussion needs to become direct or private.

## Profiles and Garage
Profiles and Garage are the identity layer. They help people show who they are, what they own, and what they care about.

## Auctions
Auctions are the live marketplace layer. They support bidding, reserve context, and listing activity.

## Sell
Sell is the seller-facing entry into listing and seller workflows. Seller tools remain intentionally web-first for now.

## How these surfaces relate
- Carmunity gives the platform its social layer
- Discussions provide structured public conversation
- Messages provide direct private conversation
- Profiles and Garage carry identity across the system
- Auctions provide real marketplace activity
- Sell and seller tools support listing creation, seller planning, and marketing workflows
