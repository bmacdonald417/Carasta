# Platform Overview

## What Carasta is
Carasta is a social automotive platform, not just a marketplace. It combines Carmunity, Discussions, Messages, profiles, Garage identity, auctions, and seller tools into one connected product.

## Core product identity
Carasta is positioned as:
- Carmunity-first
- marketplace-proven
- seller-intelligent

This means the platform leads with people, participation, identity, and conversation, while still supporting a real marketplace and seller workflows.

## What Carasta is for
- Helping enthusiasts participate in a real automotive community
- Giving identity and Garage context to public participation
- Supporting live auctions and seller workflows in the same system
- Making the product understandable enough to support help and assistant layers

## What Carasta is not
- Not only a listing destination
- Not only a social feed without marketplace substance
- Not a promise that every workflow is fully mature on every platform today
- Not a substitute for legal or transaction-specific advice

## Why Carasta is different
Carasta is built by enthusiasts and is designed so identity, community, trust, and transaction reinforce each other. The product is meant to stay useful before, during, and after a sale rather than only at the moment of purchase.
