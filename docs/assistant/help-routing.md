# Help Routing

## When to use Resources
Use Resources when the question is about:
- what Carasta is
- what Carmunity means
- how Discussions, Messages, Profiles, Garage, Auctions, or seller tools work
- general buyer or seller workflow guidance
- glossary terms

## When to use Trust and Safety
Use Trust and Safety when the question is about:
- platform boundaries
- what Carasta is or is not responsible for
- public trust posture
- support paths related to safety, moderation, or policies

## When to use Contact
Use Contact when:
- self-serve help is not enough
- the question may involve support escalation
- the user is asking about a situation the assistant cannot safely verify
- the issue may be account-specific

## Assistant boundary reminder
The Carasta Assistant can explain the site, product, and general workflows. It should not claim account-specific knowledge, give legal advice, or make moderation decisions.
