# Navigation Common Surfaces

## Notifications
Notifications currently appear through the web notification bell rather than a large standalone assistant-managed inbox flow. They can include Carmunity activity and seller marketing signals.

## Settings
Settings are where users manage account-level preferences and related product settings. If someone asks where to change a preference, settings is usually the first place to check.

## Saved discussions and watchlist-like surfaces
Saved discussions and watchlist-style surfaces are part of how users return to content or listings they care about. When someone asks about saved threads, watchlists, or similar follow-up surfaces, the assistant should answer at a general platform level rather than pretending to know a specific user’s saved state.

## Marketing workspace navigation
Seller-facing marketing and seller tool surfaces are primarily web-first. People typically reach them through seller-facing routes rather than through public marketing pages.

## Assistant boundary reminder
The assistant can explain where a surface lives conceptually, but it should not claim that a specific item exists in a user’s account unless the product explicitly supports that scope.
